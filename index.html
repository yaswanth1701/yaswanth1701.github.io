Yaswanth Gonna

I am a robotics engineer at Addverb India in Advanced Robotics department. My research interests roughly revolve around combining learning and traditional motion planning and control approachs for efficient and real-time performance. I am currenlty working on software development, motion planning and control for manipulation.

Previously I collabrated with Dr. Steve Peters to develop a simulator agnostic benchmarking suite for robotics physics engines as a part of Google Summer of Code 2024 at Open Source Robotics Foundation (OSRF). I was also an undergraduate research intern at National University of Singapore (NUS) in Multi-Agent Robotic Motion Laboratory (MARMOT), where I worked on the topic of informative footfall planning for legged robots under the guidance of Prof. Guillaume A Sartoretti.

I completed my bachelors of technology in Chemical Engineering from Vivesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT) Nagpur, India. During my undergraduate I was a part of IvLabs, where I worked under Dr. Shital Chiddhwar and started to develop my interest for robotics.
